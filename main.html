I'm a Grade 11 student from Nepal building at the intersection of software, hardware & robotics. From developing Python CLI tools and designing PCBs in EasyEDA to creating CAD models in Fusion 360, I love turning ideas into real projects.

I'm the Secretary of the Robotics Club of Valmiki and an active Hack Club member. Alongside building projects, I enjoy organizing events and communities in high school. I’ve helped organize events like Build Guild Bharatpur, Valmiki Fiesta, Tech Expo 3.0 & 4.0, Math Hunt, and Farewell programs for seniors.

Beyond technical work, I'm constantly pushing myself through competitions, leadership and collaboration. I won the Best Robo Design Award at CELESTA, an international event organized by IIT Patna, and earned the title of “Best Strategist” in my first-ever MUN. I’ve also participated in national hackathons, workshops and engineering programs that continue to expand my skills and perspective.

My goal is to study Computer Science abroad and become a software developer while continuing to build meaningful projects, communities and ideas that live beyond the screen.

⚡ Currently building: Python CLI tools, CAD projects in Fusion 360, PCB designs, robotics concepts & Hack Club Construct projects.
